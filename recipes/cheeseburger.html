Cheeseburger

Classic Cheeseburger recipe, simple classic food that everyone likes

Learn how to make the best possible cheeseburgers usign the recipe below, just follow these easy instructions

Ingredients

  • Burger Patties
  • Minced mix of beef and pork
  • Cheese of your liking
  • Ketchup
  • Pickles, Onion, Tomato

Steps

  1. Sear the meat over high flame, till its cooked
  2. Cover the patty with ketchup or any other sauce of your liking
  3. Assemble the Burger patty tomato pickles meat cheese onion patty

Now you are done, enjoy your food!
